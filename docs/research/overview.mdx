Research and architectural decisions for SiafuDB. These documents are rawer than the polished guides — they capture the *why* behind the engine choices, the fragment model, and the Graph Sync Protocol.

Read these when you want to understand why SiafuDB is built on GrafeoDB rather than forking KuzuDB, why personal and network fragments are physically separate on every device, and why the sync protocol's training loop lives inside NTL rather than in the database.
