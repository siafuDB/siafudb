SiafuDB is licensed Apache 2.0 and stewarded by the **Mukoko Foundation**, an African-led open-source foundation that holds the project's intellectual property, trademarks, and release process.

## Why a foundation

Core infrastructure should not live inside a single company. A foundation gives SiafuDB:

- A **neutral home** for the trademark and the Apache 2.0 IP grant.
- A **release process** that does not depend on any one maintainer or employer.
- A **governance model** that survives the founders — if Nyuchi Africa disappears tomorrow, SiafuDB does not.

## Scope

The foundation holds the same posture for NTL and other core Nyuchi-originated infrastructure. Product-layer projects (Mukoko super-app, end-user applications) remain inside Nyuchi Africa.

## Membership

Opening. Seats on the technical steering committee are earned through sustained contribution, not purchase. Financial sponsorship exists separately and does not buy votes.

## Licensing

- **Code:** Apache 2.0.
- **Documentation:** CC BY 4.0.
- **Trademarks:** held by the foundation; use policy follows the Apache Software Foundation model.
